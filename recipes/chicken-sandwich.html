Chicken Sandwich

Easy chicken sandwich made from frozen chicken patties found in the store.

Ingredients

  • Chicken patty
  • bread
  • Sauce of your choice. I like bolgoi sauce.

Steps

  1. Put chicken in the airfryer for 10 minutes on the highest setting
  2. Spread sauce on both pieces of bread
  3. put chicken patty on bread.
  4. allow to cool aprox 3 min.
  5. enjoy
