Fried Rice

egg fried rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • eggs
  • soy sauce
  • chicken or pork
  • oil

Steps

  1. add oil to wok and heat till bearly smoking
  2. add egg and still till nearly cooked thru
  3. add Rice
  4. flip rice for max flavor
  5. add chicken or pork and continue to stir and flip till heated thru
  6. add soy sauce
  7. enjoy
